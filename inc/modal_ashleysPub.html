Ashleys Pub

Ashley's Pub is a new venture based in Kitsap County, USA and incorporates board gaming in a friendly pub atmosphere.

Entrepreneur Ashley Martinez approached me to ask about designing a logo for the brand which could be used for promotional materials e.g. menus, fliers, t-shirts and hoodies.

I chose an image of a traditional glass tankard and gaming dice, against a faux-wood grain background. The image needed to be simple and clear, with the tagline 'Boardom's End' - a play on the word 'board game'.
