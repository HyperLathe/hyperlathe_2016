Burning Man

Burning Man is a week-long event that takes place once a year in Black Rock Desert, Nevada. It is an eclectic expression of creativity and a melting pot of community and culture that attracts over 70,000 attendees from all over the world.

Since 2013 I have been asked to create a hi-res poster of the Great Circle Fire Conclave. On the penultimate night of the event, hundreds of fire-performers and dancers gather at the base of the 'Man' structure and it's important for them to be aware of location, safety zones etc. Since 2013 I have been asked to create a hi-res poster of the Great Circle Fire Conclave.

There are two posters created, a main map showing the location of the various regional performers, the second is a detail map indicating safety boundaries, staff areas, exit points etc. The final designs are blown up and displayed in the Fire Conclave meeting point at Burning Man each year. I have also designed laminated passes for the performers and staff.
