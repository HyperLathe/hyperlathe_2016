Payment Card Solutions

PaymentCardSolutions.com provide prepaid cards and services for a number of popular brands who specialise in global money transfers and other financial solutions.

A site redesign was required for their Bread Card product, so I was brought in to revamp the brand's online presence, focusing on a simple layout with impactful visuals and an easy to navigate UI.

The final design needed to be responsive in accordance with modern web apps, enabling a seamless stylistic transition from desktop to mobile
